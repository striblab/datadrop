Minnesota's changing veteran population
The U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs publishes data estimating how many veterans they expect will be living in each state in coming years, broken down by when they served. These projections for Minnesota, which go out to 2045, show how the largest cohort – Vietnam veterans – will decline, and post 9/11 Gulf War veterans will take over as the largest group. Of course, these projections assume the U.S. doesn’t enter another war in coming years and that military enlistment trends don’t change.
